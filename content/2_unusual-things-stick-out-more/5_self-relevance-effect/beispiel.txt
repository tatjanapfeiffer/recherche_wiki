Title: Self-relevance Effect

----

Text: <p>The&nbsp;<strong>self-reference effect</strong>&nbsp;is a tendency for people to encode information differently depending on whether they are implicated in the information. When people are asked to remember information when it is related in some way to themselves, the recall rate can be improved.</p>
