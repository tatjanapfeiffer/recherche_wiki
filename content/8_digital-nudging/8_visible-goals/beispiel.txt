Title: Visible Goals

----

Text: <p>Visible goals are viewed as the idea of present simple performance measures clearly, in order to allow people to immediately assess their performance in order to visualize their goals (Lidwell, Holden &amp; Butler, 2012). Consequently, goals and performance status must be clearly visible for the user (Lidwell, Holden &amp; Butler, 2012).
