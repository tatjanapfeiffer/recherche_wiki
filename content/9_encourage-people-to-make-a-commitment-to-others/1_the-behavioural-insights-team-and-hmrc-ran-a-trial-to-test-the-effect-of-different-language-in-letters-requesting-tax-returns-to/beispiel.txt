Title: Commitment for Tax Letter Return

----

Text: <p>The Behavioural Insights Team and HMRC ran a trial to test the effect of different language in letters requesting tax returns to be filed. We found that by strengthening the tone of the commitment (from ‘thank you for agreeing’ to ‘you agreed’), response rates rose from 21% to 25%.</p>
