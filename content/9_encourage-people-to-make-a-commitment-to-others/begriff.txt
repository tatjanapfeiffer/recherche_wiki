Title: Encourage people to make a commitment to others

----

Definition: <p>There is often a gulf between what we state we want to do and what we actually end up doing. The most obvious example is smoking: 68% of smokers want to quit, but only 26% attempt to do so in any year. </p><p>The easiest way to ‘raise the stakes’ is to make your commitment in public, or to another person (ideally, someone whose respect you value).</p>
