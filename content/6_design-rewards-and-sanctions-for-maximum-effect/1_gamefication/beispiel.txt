Title: Gamefication

----

Text: <p>The advent of new technologies, particularly the use of mobile phone apps, has seen a rapid increase in ‘gamification’ — using games to engage users in achieving objectives.</p><p><br>The incentives here are often virtual (competition against others in your social network), but can also take the form of tangible rewards – for example the Incentives for Singapore Commuters scheme rewards commuters for moving from peak to off-peak trains.</p>
